1. Event Location

Location: Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), Ho Chi Minh City

Location 799 Nguyen Van Linh, District 7, Ho Chi Minh City

Date & Time: October 11, 2024, starting at 8:00 AM

Google Maps Link: Click to view map

2. Means of Transport

Public transport: Bus routes 1024,1028,1225 will stop near SECC.

Parking information: There is parking at SECC and the organizers have arranged 4 more nearby.

Bicycle parking: Parking area for cyclists.

3. Special Benefits for Participants

Free water station: Place at multiple points along the track.

Medical station: Available at the start, mid-course and finish.

Free event t-shirts: Pick up at registration desk for all participants.

Gift bag: Includes snacks, certificate of participation and discount coupons from sponsors.

4. Event Access

Wheelchair Access: SECC has a dedicated entrance for wheelchair users.

Instructions for guests with disabilities: Volunteers are available to assist throughout the event.

5. Safety Measures

COVID-19 prevention: Masks are mandatory in crowded areas. Hand sanitizer stations are located throughout.

Security checkpoint: Ensure safety for all participants and visitors.
