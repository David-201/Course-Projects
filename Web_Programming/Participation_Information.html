Participation Information

1. About participation

Taking part in the Charity Run 2024 is a great way to improve the lives of those in need and help build a stronger community. More than just a sporting event, this charity run offers an opportunity to bond and make a positive impact on society. Join us and be part of the change!

2. Detailed instructions on how to participate

How to register:

  1. Visit the event registration page register.
  2. Fill in personal information including: Full name, Phone number, Email.
  3. Select type of participation: Athlete, Volunteer, or Sponsor.
  4. Pay the participation fee (if any) via online payment gateway or directly at the event.
  5. Confirm registration via email or SMS.

Participant roles:

  • Athletes: Join the run with distances from 5km, 10km or 21km.
  • Volunteer: Assist with event organization, water distribution, participant guidance and safety throughout the run.
  • Sponsor: Make a financial contribution or in-kind sponsorship such as drinks, t-shirts, medals, or medical equipment for the event.

3. Time and place

Event Time: October 11, 2024.

Start time: 8:00am (Participants need to be present by 6:30am for check-in).

Location: Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), District 7, Ho Chi Minh City.

4. Rules and instructions for participation

  • Conditions of participation: Participants must be 18 years of age or older, in good health, and free of any medical problems that would prevent physical activity.
  • Prepare clothes: Participants should wear comfortable clothing, sneakers and bring a hat/cap for sun protection.
  • Follow the right route: Participants must follow the routes and instructions from the organizers to ensure safety.
  • Safety rules: Do not push, run in the wrong direction or cross the restricted area.
  • Health Guarantee: Drink plenty of water before and during the run to stay hydrated, and notify the volunteer immediately if you feel unwell.
