About Us

Welcome to the Charity Run 2024 event page! We are proud to bring together runners, volunteers, and sponsors in a community-driven effort to make a difference. The Charity Run 2024 is organized by Typhoon Yagi Support Network, a non-profit organization dedicated to providing relief and rebuilding efforts for northern Vietnam communities affected by Typhoon Yagi. Our mission is to promote solidarity and raise funds to support these communities in their recovery.

Our Mission

At Typhoon Yagi Support Network, we believe in the power of community. Our goal is to not only host an engaging event but also to create lasting impact by raising funds for families and individuals who have been severely affected by Typhoon Yagi. By joining the Charity Run 2024, you become part of a movement dedicated to bringing hope and support to those in need.
